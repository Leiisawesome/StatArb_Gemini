Papertest Suite (core_engine-wired)
==================================

Purpose
-------
Provide a desk-grade "papertest" suite that validates end-to-end trading
behavior in an event-driven, streaming mode using historical replay as a
realtime OHLCV feed surrogate.

Key Principles
--------------
- Thin orchestration: reuse core_engine components; avoid re-implementing logic.
- Deterministic ordering: DeterministicEventDispatcher is the authoritative bus.
- Bar-policy parity: signals computed at bar close; executed at next bar open.
- Governance: all trades go through CentralRiskManager (6-gate) in papertest v1.

Primary Flow
------------
Replay -> FeedAdapter -> ReplayToDispatcherBridge -> DeterministicEventDispatcher
-> PaperTradingEngine -> StrategyManager -> CentralRiskManager(6-gate)
-> StreamingSignalManager -> OMS -> UnifiedExecutionEngine(PAPER) -> PaperBrokerAdapter
-> Fill callback -> Dispatcher(FILL) -> PaperTradingEngine._process_fill -> Risk position update
